My Perfect Plate is a smart meal subscription platform designed to make healthy eating effortless, personalized, and consistent. Built with busy individuals in mind, it combines user preferences, nutrition goals, and AI-assisted planning to deliver curated meals straight to your door ‚Äî 365 days a year.

My Perfect Plate helps users overcome the daily decision fatigue around food. Through an intuitive onboarding flow, users set their taste preferences, dietary needs, and delivery schedules. A weekly plan is generated for approval, with flexibility to swap meals or customize ingredients. I led the product experience from early-stage UX research to user journey mapping and feature validation. I also collaborated with design and dev teams to shape core flows including meal plan approvals, recipe integration, and delivery scheduling.

Many food delivery services lacked true personalization and long-term sustainability. The market needed a platform that offered not just convenience, but consistency, health-focused value, and a frictionless experience ‚Äî without sacrificing user control or flexibility.

Perfect Plate introduced a subscription-based model supported by smart automation, real-time customization, and human-powered culinary excellence. The platform empowers users to trust the system, save time, and eat better ‚Äî with zero guesswork.
