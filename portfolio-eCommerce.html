Venetio Ceramics App

Venetio Ceramics – Bringing Artisanal Craft to Digital Shelves

Venetio Ceramics is a modern eCommerce platform designed to showcase and sell handcrafted ceramic products through an elevated, user-first experience. Blending minimalist design with practical features, the app helps a traditional craft-based business thrive in a digital environment.

Venetio Ceramics offers a curated shopping experience for buyers looking for authentic, handmade ceramics. I contributed to transforming a catalog-heavy website into a digital storefront optimized for discoverability, product storytelling, and mobile engagement. I worked closely with the team to define user flows, create filtering logic for browsing collections, and align product detail pages with brand storytelling.

The brand needed to transition from a static product showcase to a functional, responsive shopping experience — without losing its artisanal feel. The platform had to be easy to manage internally, while helping customers find, filter, and purchase products across multiple handcrafted categories.

We designed a scalable and elegant product platform that emphasized visual storytelling and usability. Customers could browse collections, filter by material, price, or style, and view products with high-resolution zoom and dynamic descriptions. The admin backend supported inventory tagging, stock tracking, and CMS updates.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Product Catalog with Filters (Material, Style, Price)
  • Artisan Profiles Linked to Product Pages
  • Seamless Add-to-Cart & Checkout Experience
  • CMS-Managed Homepage Promotions
  • Mobile-First Responsive Design
  • Product Zoom, Favorites, and “Recently Viewed”
  • Inventory Management Dashboard
  • Instagram Integration for Live Inspiration
